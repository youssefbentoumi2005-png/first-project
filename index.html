Our Story

An Elegant Oasis of Taste & Comfort

Welcome to Café Restaurant Carré d'Or, Béni Mellal's premium destination for coffee enthusiasts and food lovers alike. Located centrally on the lively Boulevard Hassan II, we combine a warm, sophisticated Moroccan aesthetic with world-class menus and service.

Whether you are beginning your day with a rich Moroccan breakfast, holding a business meeting, enjoying a wood-fired pizza with family, or winding down late at night, our doors are open around the clock to welcome you.
